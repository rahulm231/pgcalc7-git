Corporate Giving

A corporate partnership with Baylor Health Care System Foundation provides a valuable opportunity for you to align your brand with a trusted and highly recognizable cause while helping Baylor provide safe, quality, compassionate care for patients.

We have a bold vision that requires investments from across our community and corporate contributions play a critical role. With a wide range of events, programs and philanthropic opportunities designed for corporate support, there are many ways to customize your company’s involvement and advance health care for those in our community.

For more information on corporate giving and sponsorship opportunities, please contact Tim Moore at 214.820.7877 or Timothy.Moore@BaylorHealth.edu.
