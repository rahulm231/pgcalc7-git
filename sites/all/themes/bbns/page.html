Gift Planning

Credit Card

Giving to BB&N by credit card is secure, easy, and fast. Use your Visa, Mastercard, or American Express card to make your gift using our online giving form.

If you prefer, you can make a gift by credit card by calling us at 617-800-2724. Please have the following information ready when you call: credit card number, expiration date, gift amount, and what youâd like your gift to support.

Gift Planning

As you plan to provide for your familyâs future, please remember that you can extend your support by planning a gift that makes a lasting impact on BB&N and also benefits you and/or your loved ones.

  • You can provide for BB&N in your will.
  • You can receive quarterly payments in return for your gift through a charitable gift annuity.
  • You can donate your house, continue to live there, and receive a tax break.

Learn more about these and other options on our Planned Giving page.
