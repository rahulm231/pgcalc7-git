Ways to Give

There are many ways to give to Becker College.   If you need assistance or have questions about your gift, please call 508.373.9531 between 8:30 a.m.-5:00 p.m. (EST), or email giving@becker.edu.

Outright Gifts

Credit Card

Give online or call our office at the information above. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express on our secure site. You will receive an email confirmation of your tax deductible gift, followed by a receipt by mail.

Check

Make your check payable to Becker College and send it to:

Becker College
Office of Advancement
61 Sever Street
Worcester, MA 01609

Stocks, Bonds and Mutual Funds

Giving appreciated securities is a tax-wise way to support Becker. For securities held longer than one year, the full fair market value of the stock may qualify for a charitable contribution income tax deduction regardless of what you originally paid for them. You also avoid paying capital gains tax on the transfer. To make a gift of securities or mutual funds please contact Jennifer DeMaio, Director of Development at 508.373.9537 with the following information:

  • Name of the stock
  • Number of shares
  • Designation of the gift
  • Broker’s name and phone number

Matching Gifts

Does your company have a matching gift program? Ask your human resources department about matching your charitable donation, and submit the proper form when making your gift to Becker College.

To Give or Pledge by Phone

Call our office at 508.373.9531 between 8:30 a.m.-5:00 p.m. (EST). You may also hear from us during our annual Phonathon—a time when current students reach out to alumni in support of Becker’s Annual Fund. Show your support by encouraging a student caller with a gift next time you hear from us. We appreciate the warm reception and kind support they continue to receive. Note: All pledges must be paid by June 30 to count towards the current fiscal year.

Other Ways to Give

Recurring Gifts: To Give Throughout the Year

The recurring gift option provides a convenient and efficient way to spread out your annual gift over an entire year through monthly installments.

It’s easy. You decide how much you’d like to give to Becker College and whether you’d like it paid by debit/credit card or directly from a checking or savings account. We’ll continue to deduct the monthly amount you specify until you tell us otherwise.

You can specify the start date and which day of the month the deduction occurs. Recurring gifts can be set up, changed, or cancelled at any time by contacting the Office of Institutional Advancement & Alumni Relations at giving@becker.edu or calling 508.373.9531.

Sample breakdown:

$5 per month = $60 per year
$10 per month = $120 per year
$21 per month = $252 per year
$42 per month = $504 per year
$84 per month = $1,000 per year
$210 per month = $2,500 per year

One-Time Gift or Annual Pledge

By choosing the one-time gift option, you are authorizing the College to make a one-time credit card charge. Your tax-deductible donation will allow Becker College to remain competitive with other institutions while continuing to reach new heights. Click here to make a one-time credit card gift.

Gift Planning

Planned gifts are an excellent way to provide future support for the College while also helping to meet your personal financial goals. Contact Mary Maloney at (508) 373-9525 or visit our Planned Giving site for more information.

Bequests

When you remember Becker College in your will, you’ll continue supporting our mission long after you are gone. We hope you will consider including a gift to Becker College in your will or living trust. Called a charitable bequest, this type of gift is a great way to leave a lasting impact on the College in your honor.

Charitable Gift Annuities

The Becker College Charitable Gift Annuity Program is another way to make a gift to benefit Becker College. Alumni and friends of the College can make a gift of cash, securities, or other assets. Donors are then paid an annuity—a fixed percentage of the value of the gift—each year for life. After the lifetime of a donor, the remainder of the gift is donated to Becker College through the Charitable Gift Annuity.

Gifts-in-kind

Becker College accepts gifts-in-kind that are appropriate to its mission. Gifts-in-kind may include, but are not limited to, works of art, scientific equipment, books, real estate, etc. Inquiries about prospective gifts may be directed to Jennifer DeMaio at 508.373.9537.
