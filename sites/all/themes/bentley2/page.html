Gift Planning

Planned gifts provide multiple avenues for donors to support Bentley while also realizing excellent tax and financial planning benefits for themselves and loved ones. Planned gifts can be made through outright contributions, via trusts and bequests, or in some combination of financial instruments. Review our planned giving options.
