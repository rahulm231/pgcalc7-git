The Advancement Office

Bishop Feehan High School has an Advancement Office that is committed to “advancing” the mission of the school, which involves financial support, public relations and community involvement.  As a parent, alum, or friend you are asked to partner with us in one, if not all three of these goals.

As a Catholic school, Bishop Feehan does not receive any federal or state funding; therefore, it must charge tuition to exist. As a Catholic school committed to providing a quality education to all who desire it, and not just those who can afford it, Feehan’s tuition ranks it as one of the most affordable schools in New England!
 
What makes Bishop Feehan even more amazing is the fact that the quality of its teachers, programs and facilities is second to none—anywhere! Which begs the question, “How can Feehan do it?”
 
The answer is simple: sound fiscal management, resourcefulness, and an Advancement Office that solicits support from its alumni, benefactors and parents. The additional revenue that we receive from these sources is what allows Feehan to elevate the quality of its product from good to excellent!
