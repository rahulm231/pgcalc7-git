Your gift. Your way. Your power to transform.

The Bryn Mawr Fund is the College's Annual Giving program. Your annual gift to The Bryn Mawr Fund provides students with a one-of-a-kind, transformative education and supports the Bryn Mawr programs and initiatives that matter most to you.

Direct your Bryn Mawr Fund gift to an area of impact:

  • Opportunities (scholarship, internship, and research funding)
  • Innovations (academic and faculty funding)
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM)
  • Bryn Mawr's greatest needs

Bryn Mawr counts on your support each and every year.
