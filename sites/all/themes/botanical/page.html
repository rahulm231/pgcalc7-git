Gift Planning
Commemorative Giving Program

The Garden's commemorative tribute gift program is a wonderful way to honor family and friends throughout the year.

A personal acknowledgment is sent to the family or individual being honored, without reference to the size of the gift. Tributes of more than $50 will be listed in the Garden's Bulletin.

Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings and engagements, graduations, Mother's Day and Father's Day—or as an expression of sympathy.

You may give a commemorative tribute gift using our online form. If you would prefer to make a tribute gift by mail, please send your check to:

Missouri Botanical Garden
Commemorative Gift Fund
P.O. Box 299
St. Louis, MO 63166-0299

If you have questions regarding the Commemorative Gift Program or would like to request a commemorative tribute envelope, please call (314) 577-5118 or email membership@mobot.org.
