Planned Giving

The Corporate and Foundation Relations team works to build strong ties with businesses and foundations across the country to help enhance the care provided throughout Baylor Scott & White Health - Central Texas. These mutually beneficial relationships allow companies, organizations, and individuals to have a lasting impact on Baylor Scott & White and the patients we serve. 

As a partner with the Baylor Scott & White - Central Texas Foundation, you can help make a difference in the lives of 2.5 million patients throughout Central Texas. Your support is vital as we strive to be the most trusted name in giving and receiving safe, quality, compassionate healthcare.

Foundations

Foundations are at the heart of philanthropy. Baylor Scott & White Health depends on the generosity of foundations to help enhance our numerous programs and projects, from research to capital projects, continuing education, charity care, and beyond. Our patients, staff, and community benefit from the power of philanthropy, today and in the future.

Partner with us

 

Corporations

Philanthropy plays a major part in many of our most exciting innovations and patient services. You can become a partner with Baylor Scott & White and make a difference in our patients' lives. Contact our team to discuss how to best match your philanthropic goals with the funding opportunities at Baylor Scott & White Health - Central Texas.

Corporate Circle

Through the Corporate Circle program, our team seeks to create strategic partnerships with impact to help mee the needs of patients throughout the Baylor Scott & White Health - Central Texas division. Some benefits available to Corporate Circle program participants may include:

  • Increased visibility for your organization among our researchers, physicians and program directors, as well as brand recognition through co-branding with Baylor Scott & White.
  • Awareness of system initiatives, such as research, education, and outreach programs, and of opportunities to partner with us, such as special events or gift matching efforts
  • Donor recognition (varies according to cumulative giving) such as naming opportunities, donor wall listing, custom plaque, check presentation photo opportunity, news release submitted to local media outlets, and luncheon with executive leadership

Corporate Circle has several giving levels which determine the benefits corporations may receive:

  • Visionary ($10,000,000+)
  • Benefactor ($1,000,000 - $9,999,999.99)
  • Patron ($100,000 - $999,999.99)
  • Philanthropist ($10,000 - $99,999.99)
  • Advocate ($5,000 - $9,999.99)
