Friends of Colburn

The Friends of Colburn groups play a vital role in the Colburn School community, helping to ensure excellence in the performing arts now and for years to come.

The Friends of Colburn serve the Colburn School community by providing the School's parents, students, friends, and supporters a chance to get together to help each other, the school, and most importantly our students. The Friends are a committed group of people who participate in the Colburn School community through contributions of time, effort, and financial support.

The Friends also provide a means of support for the efforts of the Colburn School to have a meaningful presence in the different communities of Los Angeles, through performances and other gatherings in those communities. The Friends work to cultivate understanding and support for the mission of the school through its work in those communities.

In gratitude for their efforts, the Colburn School is committed to supporting the activities of all of the Friends of Colburn groups.
