  • Why Your Support Matters

The Visitation Fund, the school’s annual fund, provides vital operational support across all areas of school life. As one of the many support tools that helps Georgetown Visitation provide its students with a top-notch education and experience, the Visitation Fund is critical to our success. Without our community’s support of the annual fund, the school could not provide our students with the all educational and co-curricular opportunities that help them thrive.

Did you know?

  • Students’ tuition does not fully cover the cost of their education.
  • Your gifts help close this gap and help us continue shaping lives and building bright futures.
  • All members of the community are urged to contribute annually.
  • The monies raised directly impact every student’s education and form a vital part of the school’s operating budget.

Please join other parents, alumnae, past parents, faculty, friends, and grandparents who send the message that they are committed to Visitation! Your contribution to The Visitation Fund ensures that our shared values and way of life endure.
