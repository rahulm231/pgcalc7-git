Planned Giving

Your Gift for Families Will Help:

  • Fulfill our promise and inspire others to share in the mission and support the vital work of Holy Cross Family Ministries.
  • Welcome others to experience the power and promise of family prayer and spiritual support.
  • Offer opportunities for people around the world to strengthen their faith and spirituality through our many programs and services.
  • Serve as a visible sign of your belief in the importance of inspiring, educating, and entertaining others as our Founder, Venerable Peyton, has inspired you.
