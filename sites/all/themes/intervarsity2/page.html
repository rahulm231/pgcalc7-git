About Giving

Join us!

Be a part of InterVarsity’s ministry to students and faculty on over 680 campuses nationwide.

InterVarsity is primarily a donor-supported organization that relies on the gifts of people like you to fund its ministry to over 40,000 core students and faculty. In addition to financial contributions, we welcome support from alumni and volunteers around the country who help us reach more students and faculty with the gospel.

According to IRS guidelines, InterVarsity/USA must maintain sole control and discretion over the use of the funds once a tax-deductible receipt is issued. Tax deductible gifts are non-refundable. In the event that changes occur in InterVarsity staff, ministries or projects, all tax-deductible gifts remain non-refundable.
