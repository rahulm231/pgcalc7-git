Support

66 performances, 42 dancers, and countless pirouettes. Your support is what makes every Joffrey moment possible. 

 

As we celebrate Ashley Wheater's 10th anniversary as Artistic Director, we asked him and our Company Artists to share their thoughts on the meaning of donor support.

 

Luis Vazquez: Empowering Rising Stars

The Joffrey took a chance on me at two pivotal points in my career. Now I'm giving back thanks to your support. 

 

Nicolas Blanc: You Give Artists a Voice

Your gift gives power to working artists and keeps the art form moving forward. 

 

Ashley Wheater: Share Our Journey

Your support brings the best dancers, choreographers, and artists to Chicago each season, which is part of what makes the Company so innovative. 

 

Fabrice Calmels: Strengthen the Joffrey's Future

To be a dancer takes training, commitment, and perseverance. Your gift ensures that talent is nurtured from one generation to the next. 

 

Jeraldine Mendoza: Be a Part of the Joffrey Family

At the Joffrey, everyone supports each other, the way a family should. As a donor, you are part of our family. 

 

Dylan Gutierrez: Keep the Joffrey at the Top of Our Game

Support from friends like you has helped us grow into world-class cultural ambassadors. With your gift, we can do even more.
