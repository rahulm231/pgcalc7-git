Gift Planning

Martin Health Foundation

Since 1939, Martin Health System has provided high-quality, award-winning, affordable health care to our community. And because we’re a nonprofit, the gifts from our friends, former patients and families are what keep us here to serve the next generation of patients—thank you!

If you are looking to help ensure future excellence and longevity at MHS while furthering your own personal objectives, gift planning is the perfect way to help. Gift planning not only benefits Martin Health System, but these kinds of gifts can also greatly benefit you through tax savings. Depending on the gift option that you choose, you may also receive cash payments that can supplement retirement income or provide support for family members and friends.

Gift planning options include:

  • Bequests
  • Retirement Plan Gifts
  • Life Insurance Gifts
  • Charitable Gift Annuities
  • Charitable Remainder Trusts
  • Gifts of Real Estate
  • Other Arrangements

When considering this type of gift, start by asking how you want to benefit Martin Health System and how you'd like to benefit yourself and others. How would your gift make the biggest impact to both you and MHS? Once the purpose of the gift has been determined, then the practical aspects such as the gift assets to be used, the financial benefits to be secured, and the documentation of the gift's purposes can be worked out. We can help you find the best way to make your gift and perhaps find a gift option that you might not have otherwise thought was possible.

While a gift through your long-term plans can often be relatively simple to arrange, you should always seek the advice and guidance of your attorney, accountant, or other appropriate advisor. We, at Martin Health System, would be happy to discuss the various giving options with you and your advisors.
