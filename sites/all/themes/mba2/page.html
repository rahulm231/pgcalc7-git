Ways to Give


Easy Ways to Give Today

1. Debit and Credit Card:
It is easy and secure. Make a gift with your VISA or MasterCard by clicking here. (Gifts by American Express can be made by phone)


Give Online

2. Send a Check:

Make checks payable to:

Montgomery Bell Academy
Attn: Development Office
4001 Harding Road
Nashville, TN 37205

3. Make a Pledge
Not ready to give? Make a pledge. Gifts are frequently made in the form of a pledge, payable in installments throughout the year. Many people find that they can make a larger gift by spreading their payments over a number of months. The Annual Fund year begins on July 1 and ends on June 30.


4. Gift of Securities:
MBA has accounts with many of the area brokerage houses. Click here to get instructions and to select a house. Upon selection, please contactJody Johnsonat (615) 369-5366 orJody.Johnson@montgomerybell.edu.


Click here for instructions


5. Give Monthly:
Monthly giving is an easy way to spread your gift over a year. Consider a gift of $25, $50 or $100 per month.

6. Matching Gifts:
Many companies offer programs that match gifts made by their employees. If you think your (or your spouses) company has a matching program, contact our office.

Contact Us

To make a gift or pledge by phone or if you have any questions, contact Millicent Van Mol Vance at (615)369-5362 or millicent.vance@montgomerybell.edu.

 

 

Montgomery Bell Academy
