Giving

A tiny mustard seed can grow into a 20-foot shade tree. And a small donation has the power to grow into something astounding.

Your gifts to the Seminary show our students and faculty that you have faith in them and what they can achieve. Giving to the Annual Fund, developing Scholarships, and donating to mini-campaigns make it possible for more people to answer the call to serve.

Planned Giving

Plan-a-gift will help you determine the best way for you to give based on your age, goals, and assets. The Hughes family chose to created an endowed scholarship.

Special Occasions

Celebrate someone you love or a special occasion. In memory of her mother, Dr. Michelle Santiago created a prize for a graduating MACC student whose clinical practice and application of psychology betters the human condition.
