Support Us


Support Us

The Morris Hospital Auxiliary and Foundation share a mission to engage the community in philanthropic support for Morris Hospital. Both organizations provide for programs and equipment purchases which may not otherwise be possible for the Hospital or would require more time for the Hospital to fund through its operations.

Each governed by a volunteer Board of Directors, the Auxiliary and Foundation receive gifts of all sizes from individuals, local businesses, grantors, employees and physicians, grateful patients and their families, and others who are invested in the health of our community.

Auxiliary and Foundation Highlights from 2014:

  • Raised more than $14,000 for Morris Hospital's Pediatrics Security System at our inaugural Wild West Fest
  • More than 600 volunteers provided 56,000 hours of service for our hospital
  • Purchased 45 new Patient Care Carts thanks to the $95,000 that was raised at our annual Auxiliary Gala and more than $90,000 that was raised through our year end community appeal
  • Provided for new LifeLine® units, which will enable more people to live independently in their homes, and a Patient Transportation Bus which provides complimentary, non-emergent rides to health-related appointments, with proceeds from the annual Auxiliary Golf Outing and Bridge/Euchre Tournament
  • Gave $13,000 in scholarships for students pursuing an education in healthcare
  • Distributed $58,000 of grant funding for health-related programs and equipment for local agencies and rescue squads
  • Helped employees and volunteers experiencing a disaster or hardship through our Relief Fund
  • Provided tranquility for patients, families, and staff in our Serenity Garden
  • Supported Camp 911, a popular program teaching emergency skills and first aid to 100 kids each summer
  • Community support and grants to the Auxiliary and the Foundation totaled $763,000, making it our best year ever!

Would you like to help make some of these amazing things possible? If so, consider making a gift to the Foundation conveniently and securely through an online donation.
