Give Now

We are grateful for every gift no matter what the size. Whether you want to make an outright gift of cash now, or include Munson Healthcare in your estate planning, there are many ways to give. Because Munson Healthcare, Cadillac Hospital Foundation, Charlevoix Hospital Foundation, and Paul Oliver Foundation are nonprofit organizations, all gifts are tax deductible. Please consult your CPA or tax planner to understand personal deductibility.


Donate Now

The Munson Healthcare Foundations, receive many kind donations every day. Some are from grateful patients, family, friends, and neighbors. Some gifts are paying forward the kindness of others. Others are to honor a loved one's memory. By choosing to donate now your gift will make an immediate impact on patients receiving health care in our region.

Grateful Patient

If you or your family is looking for a way to say "thank you" to a staff member who has shown expertise, compassion, or otherwise went above and beyond, please consider making a donation in their honor. Click here to make a gift.
     
   

Monthly or Continuous Giving

There are many donors who would like to make a large gift, but writing a check is not financially feasible. That is why we offer monthly giving. By giving a donation every month, it can slowly add up at a more comfortable pace. To give monthly, please click here and complete the requested information. Or, simply call us at (231) 935-6482.
     
   

Pledges

If it is more convenient for you to make your gift over time, we are happy to receive major gifts in pledges to be paid over time up to five years. We will be happy to meet with you and discuss your options. To make an appointment with a giving specialist, please call (231) 935-6482.
     
   

Support or Plan an Event

Please click here to complete the form. You may also show your support by attending an upcoming event in our area.
     
   

Stock or Mutual Funds

Gifts of stock or mutual funds are among the most mutually-beneficial and easiest ways to give to your local hospital within the Munson Healthcare System. Gifts of stock or mutual funds are eligible for a tax deduction for the full market value on the date of the gift. Also, the donor avoids paying capital gains on the stock or real estate's increased value. Please consult your attorney or financial adviser about gifts of stock or mutual funds. To complete a stock transfer, please complete this form. 
     
   

Honorarium

A gift in honor of someone special is a wonderful way of commemorating a special occasion. "In Honor of" gifts are a meaningful way to observe someone's birthday, anniversary, or other special occasions. To make a gift in a loved one's or friend's honor, please click here.
     
   

Gifts in Kind

Many material items or services may also be donated and are always appreciated. Please call to discuss the items or services you would like to gift in kind. Your generosity is appreciated. Please call (231) 935-6482.
