                • Ways to Give

                We are grateful for gifts of every size. Whether you want to make an outright gift of cash now, or include Munson Healthcare in your legacy planning, there are many ways to give. Because Munson Healthcare and its entities are nonprofit organizations, all gifts are tax deductible. Please consult your CPA or tax planner to understand personal deductibility.

                Donate Now

                By choosing to donate now, your gift will make an immediate impact on patients receiving health care in our region. Donate to:

                • Cadillac Hospital
                • Cancer Services
                • Charlevoix Hospital
                • Grayling Hospital
                • Heart Services
                • Manistee Hospital
                • Munson Healthcare Hospice
                • Munson Manor Hospitality House
                • Munson Medical Center
                • MMC Family Birth and Children’s Center
                • Otsego Memorial Hospital
                • Patient Needs
                • Paul Oliver Memorial Hospital

                Grateful Patient

                If you are looking for a way to say "thank you" to a staff member who has shown expertise, compassion, or otherwise went above and beyond, please consider making a donation in their honor. Select one of the options above to make your gift now.

                Monthly Giving

                Many donors want to make a large gift, but writing a check is not financially feasible. That is why we offer monthly giving. By giving a donation every month, it can slowly add up at a more comfortable pace. To give monthly, please complete the requested form. Or, simply call us at 231-935-6482.

                Pledges

                If it is more convenient for you to make your gift over time, we are happy to receive major gifts in pledges to be paid over time up to five years. We will be happy to meet with you and discuss your options. To make an appointment with a giving specialist, please call 231-935-6482.

                Support or Plan an Event

                Please complete our application form. You may also show your support by attending an upcoming event in our area.

                Stock or Mutual Funds

                Gifts of stock or mutual funds are among the most mutually-beneficial and easiest ways to give to your local hospital. Gifts of stock or mutual funds are eligible for a tax deduction for the full market value on the date of the gift. Also, the donor avoids paying capital gains on the stock or real estate's increased value. Please consult your attorney or financial adviser about gifts of stock or mutual funds. To complete a stock transfer, please complete this form.

                Memorials and Honorariums

                A gift in memory or honor of someone is deeply meaningful. When a gift is made in memory or honor of a loved one or a friend, the immediate family is notified that a gift has been made to honor their loved one. “In Honor of" gifts are a meaningful way to observe someone's birthday, anniversary, or other special occasion. The Foundation sends a thank you letter to the donor acknowledging their donation. If you would like more information on ways to honor a loved one's memory, please call 231-935-6482.

                Plan Your Legacy

                When you make a legacy gift to your local hospital through your will, estate, or financial plans, the impact of your generosity lives on. Investing in the wellness of our northern Michigan communities is a wonderful way to help people enjoy healthier, better, and longer lives. To learn more, call 231-935-6448.

                Learn more about legacy giving.

                Donor Advised Fund

                A donor advised fund is a centralized vehicle for charitable giving that makes it easy for donors to dedicate funds to support their favorite nonprofit organization. It also provides a convenient and tax-efficient method for donors to manage their charitable giving.

                With a donor advised fund, charitably inclined individuals, families, and businesses may take an immediate tax deduction. Donors can recommend grants over time to IRS-qualified 501(c)(3) public charities. Most donor advised funds accept donations of long-term appreciated securities and other assets.

                Recommend a gift to Munson Healthcare now through your donor advised fund using DAF Direct. Simply enter the amount and then click next to complete your recommendation.
