<h2 id='pageHeader2'>National Lutheran Communities & Services. For retirement living. For health and senior care. For a rewarding way of life.</h2>
<div id="pageContent" style="line-height:150%"><p>National Lutheran Communities &amp; Services (NLCS) has been blessed with a long tradition of service to people of all faiths. Since 1890, we&rsquo;ve helped people find wonderful choices for retirement living, new options for financial security and expert services for health care and wellness. While staying rooted in our heritage, we have continued to plan for the needs of future generations we will serve.</p>
<p>NLCS is a faith-based, not-for-profit ministry of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America&rsquo;s (ELCA) Delaware-Maryland, Metropolitan Washington, D.C. and Virginia Synods serving people of all beliefs. NLCS provides seniors with a variety of lifestyle, residential and health care options through retirement communities in Maryland and Virginia.</p>
<p>Explore life at <a href="lutheran-retirement-rockville-maryland-md">The Village at Rockville</a>,(formerly the National Lutheran Home) in Rockville,&nbsp;Md., <a href="lutheran-retirement-staunton-virginia-va">The Legacy at North Augusta</a> in Staunton, Va., and our newest communities: <a href="lutheran-retirement-winchester-virginia-va">The Village at Orchard Ridge</a>, a CCRC in Winchester, Va., and <a href="lutheran-retirement-annapolis-maryland-md">The Village at Crystal Spring</a>, coming soon to Annapolis, Md. &nbsp;Learn about our home care services with <a href="http://www.mypotentialathome.com" target="_blank">myPotential at Home</a>.&nbsp;</p></div>
