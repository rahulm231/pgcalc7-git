One Destination... Two American Masters

Lasting impact, planned gifts – such as bequests or charitable trusts – are among the most important financial tools available to strengthen the future of the Museum. Such methods are often an attractive and appropriate alternative to outright contributions.

Ohr-O’Keefe Museum of Art welcomes a variety of planned gifts. Among the most common:

Bequests. Naming the Ohr-O’Keefe in your will with a bequest of cash, securities, Ohr pottery or other assets is a simple and easy way to bolster the future of the Museum.

Charitable Gift Annuities. Ohr-O’Keefe Museum of Art can administer charitable gift annuities (minimum contribution: $10,000) that provide you both an immediate tax deduction and a guaranteed income for life.

Other types of planned or deferred gifts include charitable remainder trusts, a gift of life insurance, or a gift of retirement plan assets such as an IRA or 401k.

For more information about planned giving opportunities, please contact the Museum.
