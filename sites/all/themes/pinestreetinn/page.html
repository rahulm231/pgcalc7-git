Other Ways to Give

 

TRIBUTE AND MEMORIAL GIFTS

A tribute or memorial gift is a particularly meaningful way to honor or remember family, friends and colleagues. Pine Street will prepare an acknowledgment card and promptly deliver it either to you or to the recipient you request.
Read more

PLANNED GIVING

Including Pine Street Inn in your bequest or other estate plans ensures that the issues you care about today will continue to receive your financial support in the future.
Read more

 

STOCK GIFTS

An outright gift of appreciated securities is a quick and easy way to make a meaningful gift and to receive an income tax deduction for the fair market value of the stock at the time of the gift.
Read more

MATCHING GIFTS

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs to match charitable contributions made by their employees. Our matching gifts page can help you find out if your company has a matching gift policy.
Read more
 

Wire transfers

Wire Transfers and ACH Transfer options allow your bank to send your gift electronically to Pine Street Inn. For account and routing information, please contact Judi Jackson at 617.892.9171.
