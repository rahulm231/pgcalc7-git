Ways to Give

We’re leaders among Pennsylvania charities.

Join with us as our partner in ensuring quality care and senior living options through a gift to Presbyterian SeniorCare Foundation. Among Pennsylvania charities, we’re known for the SeniorCARE FUND that supports benevolent care to assist residents who have outlived their resources and the ability to pay for their care.

Disclaimer: All gifts to Presbyterian SeniorCare Foundation are tax deductible as provided by law. A copy of the official registration and financial information may be obtained from the Pennsylvania Department of State by calling toll free within Pennsylvania, 800-732-0999. Registration does not imply endorsement.

  • Make a Tribute Gift

    Remembering the life of a loved one or honor a special occasion. You will receive a prompt acknowledgement of your gift; the person being honored will receive a special notification letter with no mention of the gift amount.  (For memorial gifts, the letter will be sent to the person’s family.) 

    If you would like to direct family and friends to make memorial gifts to Presbyterian SeniorCare Network, you may download and print our suggested language for your loved one's obituary. (This document is in PDF format and requires Adobe Acrobat Reader.)

    Donate now by credit card. Please click here.

    To make your gift by mail, just download and print our Donation Form. (This document is in a PDF format and requires Adobe Acrobat Reader.) Please make your check payable to Presbyterian SeniorCare Foundation.

    Close

  • Gifts of Stock

    Gifts of Stock or appreciated securities can support the Presbyterian SeniorCare Network mission and might ease your capital gains tax burden (consult your tax professional for additional information). Please contact the Foundation before making your gift of stock, as privacy laws prohibit us from knowing who transferred the stock. When you contact us personally about your gift of stock, we will ensure that you receive proper acknowledgement and that your gift is directed to the program of your choice. You may download and print our Stock Transfer Form (this document is in PDF format and requires Adobe Acrobat Reader).

    Close

  • Planned Gifts

    Planned giving is an opportunity for you to create a legacy with a deferred gift to Presbyterian SeniorCare Foundation through a bequest, retirement plan, life insurance plan, charitable gift annuity, charitable remainder trust, or a charitable lead trust.

    Your gift will ensure that Presbyterian SeniorCare Network is able to carry out our work of enhancing the quality of life in older adults for years to come. For more information please contact Nancy Hart, planned giving director, at 412-826-6087 or email her at nhart@srcare.org.   

    Close

  • Matching Gifts

    Matching Gifts enable you to double your support! Please contact the Human Resources Department at your or your spouse's employer to see if they offer this service and to obtain a matching gift form.

    Close

  • United Way and other Workplace Giving Campaigns

    United Way and other Workplace Giving Campaigns allow you to support our mission each year by designating your United Way or government campaign contribution to Presbyterian SeniorCare Network.

  • In-Kind Gifts

    In-kind gifts are non-cash gifts of products, goods or services, such as equipment, books, computers, furniture, etc. While we are grateful for all donations, your in-kind gifts may only be accepted if they are usable by Presbyterian SeniorCare Network for our residents. Please contact the Foundation to inquire if your items can benefit Presbyterian SeniorCare Network.
