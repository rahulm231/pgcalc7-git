    • Why I Give

A planned gift provides an opportunity to integrate charitable giving into your overall financial, tax and estate planning to maximize benefits for you and PSU. Planned giving also gives you peace of mind, knowing that your assets are directed to benefit the people and places you care about most.

When you make a planned gift to PSU, you leave a legacy that will ensure the university's scholarship funds, educational programming and community partnerships continue to flourish.
