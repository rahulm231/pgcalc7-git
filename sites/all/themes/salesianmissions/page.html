Planned Giving

"Income for life? We used to think it was only a dream, but then the Salesians told us about their investment charity program called the Salesian Annuity Plan."

Remember Christ's needy in your will... The Good Lord gave us everything we have. Therefore, nothing could be more fitting than to remember Him in our wills. When you include Salesian Missions in your will, you help His work in many lands... His children who need assistance... and you bring His love and His care to many people who desperately need it.

Bequests and annuities are tools you can use to maximize your gift to help the poor and minimize its impact on your own tax planning efforts.
