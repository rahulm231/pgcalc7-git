Planned Giving

Online Gifts

You can give with a credit card online using American Express, Visa, or MasterCard.

Gifts by Mail

Printable Gift Form

If you are giving by check, please make it payable to Sidwell Friends School and designate the fund name in the memo field. Mail your gift to our drop box address:

Sidwell Friends
P.O. Box 5037
White Plains, NY 10602-5037

Make a Pledge

You can make a pledge to support the School to be paid at a future date or in a series of installments.

Memorial and Tribute Gifts

A gift to Sidwell Friends is a special way to honor or remember a special person or event. A note will be sent to the individual or family you have honored; the amount of the gift will not be disclosed.

Recurring Gifts

Many donors are able to give more by spreading their gift out over the year through monthly or quarterly donations. You can determine the total amount you would like to contribute and spread it out evenly through the end of the fiscal year, or you may simply choose an amount to give each month. Make a recurring gift through our secure online giving form.

Matching Gifts

See if your company is match eligible. You may have the opportunity to double or even triple the value of your gift. Many companies offer matching gift programs to their employees as part of the benefits package. Please contact your Human Resources Office to learn more about your company’s matching gift policy and to determine if you are eligible for a matching gift, or use the link above to search the list we have compiled.
