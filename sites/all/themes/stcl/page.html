Giving Societies

South Texas College of Law’s giving societies recognize the remarkable difference our generous donors make on campus and beyond. Click the society names to learn more and view the current membership of each group.

The Spurgeon Bell Giving Society honors South Texas’s most generous annual supporters—those who contribute $1,000 or more on an annual basis.

The 1923 Society honors those donors of the law school who have generously donated a total of $25,000 all the way to $10,000,000 or more to South Texas over their lifetimes. Named for the year of South Texas founding, the 1923 Society was launched in 2013 to recognize and celebrate an elite group of donors who demonstrate a heightened commitment to our mission.

The Fred Parks Legacy Society recognizes the generosity of alumni and friends who have named South Texas as a beneficiary of a will, trust, retirement plan, or other planned gift. The society is a tribute to Fred Parks ’37, who through his estate plan made the largest individual gift in South Texas’s 93-year history.
