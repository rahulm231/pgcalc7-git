Planned Giving

Planned giving is a special way to make a lasting contribution to UUSC’s work to create a world free of oppression while achieving your financial objectives at the same time.

  • Planned Giving Options

    Through planned giving, you can help us create a world free of oppression. Learn more about the variety of options available.

  • Flaming Chalice Circle

    The Flaming Chalice Circle giving society recognizes those who include UUSC in their estate plans.

  • Contact UUSC's Planned Giving Team

    Contact our team for assistance with your planned giving questions or for more information.
