    Gift Planning

    Located on a hill overlooking the shores of Lake Champlain, at the foot of the Green Mountains, the University of Vermont is a powerhouse of innovation in healthcare, environment and conservation, sustainable business, food systems, renewable energy and arts and sciences.

    Our faculty and students make an impact on society and the environment in countless ways through innovation, passion and hard work.  From farms to hospitals, classrooms, studios and labs, UVM enriches lives, conducts ground-breaking research and produces not just graduates; but citizens and leaders who are equipped to approach the world’s largest challenges headfirst and hands on.

    The Office of Corporate and Foundation Relations at the University of Vermont is here to help you navigate the university and make introductions to faculty, staff and students.

    Exciting things are happening every day at UVM.  Let us connect you to the expertise, creativity and passion that makes UVM decidedly different.
