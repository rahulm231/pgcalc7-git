Invest in Excellence: Front & Center

The Comprehensive Campaign for Walnut Hill Exceeds its Goal

June 30, 2018 marked the end of our fiscal year, as well as the end of our comprehensive campaign, Invest in Excellence: Front & Center. It was a banner year for the Annual Fund, and a wonderful conclusion to our multi-year fundraising campaign. Thanks to your generosity, we received $1.86 million in charitable gifts to the School for the fiscal year ending June 30, including $1.15 million allocated to the Annual Fund. We are thrilled to report that this brings the campaign total to $16.3 million in cash, pledges, and commitments, exceeding our original goal of $15 million. This success would not have been possible without the generous support of our alumni, parents, and friends. 

Thank you for your investment in Walnut Hill—Your support ensures the education of young artists remains “Front & Center.”

We Need You!

You can participate in Invest in Excellence: Front & Center by making a cash gift, a gift of securities or a planned gift. Leadership gifts may be payable over a period of up to five years. For more information, please contact Marie Longo, Chief Advancement Officer (mlongo@walnuthillarts.org , 508.650.5019)

A Message from the Head of School

“We believe that art and creative thinking are essential to promoting a more tolerant, mindful, and beautiful world. The open mindset that an arts education supports is crucial in preparing student to find new solutions to the complex problems of our ever-changing world. Thank you for being a part of the Walnut Hill community. Your investment in this campaign will help us transform the world.”
-Antonio Viva, Head of School
