About Us


Established in 1999 as a not-for-profit 501(c)(3) organization, the Westminster Ingleside Foundation has looked toward the future with a focus on promoting the health and well-being of seniors. The Foundation supports the charitable activities of all Ingleside continuing care retirement communities including Ingleside at Rock Creek, Westminster at Lake Ridge, and Ingleside at King Farm.

The Foundation's mission takes two primary forms. It provides financial assistance to Ingleside community residents whose financial resources, through no fault of their own, have been exhausted. The Foundation also supports capital projects, special programs, and activities, which are deemed to have common or shared benefits for the residents of one of more of our communities.
